  1. Karachi

  1. Karachi is the largest city of Pakistan & 12th largest city of the world
  2. Karachi is the capital of province Sindh
  3. The tomb of Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah is also situated in Karachi city
  4. It has four districts
    1. Districts East
    2. Districts West
    3. Districts south
    4. Districts Central
  5. The famous foods of Karachi are Biryani,Nihari & Haleem
  6. Karachi is home to Pakistan and South Asia's largest shopping mall, Lucky One Mall which hosts more than two hundred stores.
  7. Being the largest city, Karachi is also Pakistan's financial and commercial capital.
  8. Famous places of Karachi are Frere hall, Tomb of Quaid & Seaview
