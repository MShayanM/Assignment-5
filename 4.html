Peshawar

  1. The word Peshawar means the city of men
  2. Peshawar is the capital of Khyber pakhtunkhwa
  3. Peshawar is the city that welcome travellers
  4. People have crossed the mountains to peshawar for centuries, once to fight for land & now to trade & to make their homes in Pakistan.
  5. The weather of Peshawar is hot & warm during summers.
  6. The winters are cold but not frosty
  7. Peshawar is also known for its market where one can buy the best fabrics & other household things include electronic items.
  8. There are currently 9 Medical colleges in Peshawar, 2 in public sector while 7 in private sector.
