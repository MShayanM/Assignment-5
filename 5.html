Islamabad

  1. Islamabad is the capital city of Pakistan.
  2. The major part of this modern city was built in five years starting in the year 1961.
  3. It is a well-planned city.
  4. It has wide streets with large prks & gardens.
  5. The government of Pakistan has its main offices in Islamabad.
  6. Islamabad summers are hot with an average of 300mm of rain in July & August.
  7. There is winter frost at night from december to february.
  8. The famous place of Islamabad is Faisal masjid.
